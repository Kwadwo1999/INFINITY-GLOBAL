About Us

We’re built to break and support artists around the world

INFINITY - GLOBAL Distribution is Europe's leading independent music distribution and marketing company located in London, United Kingdom. INFINITY - GLOBAL Distro provides thousands of new and established record labels, managers and individual artists with global digital music, video and podcast distribution to hundreds of retail and streaming platforms both local and international, as well as playlist pitching, release promotion, and a comprehensive suite of label services.

Some artiste we have worked with are; Bad Boy Timz, Nqobilé, Bucky Raw, Yarden and others.
